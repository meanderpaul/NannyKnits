About Nanny Knits

Welcome! I'm Laurie Reed, the hands and heart behind Nanny Knits. Knitting isn't just a hobby for me; it's a passion passed down through generations. There's nothing quite like the warmth and comfort of a hand-knitted blanket, crafted with love and care in every stitch.

Based in Plymouth, MA, I pour my creativity into making beautiful, cozy blankets that bring joy and comfort to your home. Each piece is unique and made with high-quality yarn.
